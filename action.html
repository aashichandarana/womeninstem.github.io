Join a Program

  1. Girls Who Code is an amazing nonprofit organization that aims to increase the number of women that go into computer science. They offer an in person and online course where students can learn web designing, cybersecurity, and data science and AI. This program inspires girls to work in a STEM related field.
  2. Kode With Klossy is a wonderful program that teaches scholars interested in data science. They teach high schoolers how you can use Python to arrange data and how to form your own data visualizations while also giving you the opportunity to interact with your peers.
  3. Women Who Code is also a great foundation that is more for grown women, which is just as important as forming a tech interest in younger girls. This program provides women with the right services and materials so they can pursue their technology career.

Promote the Cause

  1. The Association for Women in Science is a great association that partners with other organizations. It works to fight against low pay and job discrimination. This program offers many webinars and in-person workshops to help women develop better skills to succeed in the STEM field.
  2. The American Association of University Women is a wonderful nonprofit organization committed to fighting for gender equality in US technology fields using education, research, and advocacy. It also invests a lot in STEM education to help younger girls find an interest in this field.
  3. Million Women Mentors is a beautiful organization that provides females with opportunities to expand on their STEM knowledge. Mentors work to spark an interest in stem in young girls and create more opportunities through a network of leaders and social media.
